### How to gain Meta points

#### MCS
* Top 32 = 5 points 
* Top 16 = 8 points 
* Top 8 = 12 points 
* Top 4 = 18 points 
* Second Place = 25 points 
* First Place = 35 points

#### Meta Weekly
* Top 16 = 2 point 
* Top 8 = 3 points 
* Top 4 = 5 Points 
* Second Place = 8 points 
* First Place = 12 points

#### KC Cup
* Top 10 = 20 points
* Top 100 = 10 points
