#### Tournament Information
- Japanese Tournament
- Invite only
- Day 1 - 128 Participants: March 10th 
- Day 2 - Top 32: March 11th

#### Format
- 128 Players
- Double Elimination
- 1 Deck with a 7 card Side Deck
